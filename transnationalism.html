The documentary Brasiguaios: Transnational lives and identities is a product of the Transnationalism project, an online integrated platform created to provide scholars, students and others interested in transnationalism with information and resources to support and expand this field of study. The objective of this documentary is to present how Brasiguaio identities are upheld and represented in an array of ways; thus, there is a need to recognise the existence of multiple Brasiguaio identities, as it is not possible to speak of a single Brasiguaio identity.
